OUR HERITAGE

Crafted with Centuries
of Wisdom

Yafouh was founded on a single belief: fragrance is the most intimate form of expression. Rooted in the souk traditions of the Arabian Peninsula, each perfume is a handcrafted story.

Our master perfumers source only the finest raw ingredients — oud chips aged for decades, Bulgarian roses harvested at dawn, rare Himalayan musk — blending them into compositions that evolve beautifully on your skin.

  • Ethically sourced, sustainably crafted
  • No synthetic shortcuts — only pure oil blends
  • Cruelty-free, certified by IFRA standards
